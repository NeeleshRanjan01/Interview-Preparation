5.Difference between block and inline

A block element take full width of the screen or webpage and always begins with a new line. Browser automatically add some space before and after the element. Examples : h1 , p, div, table, form etc. whereas inline element does not begin with a new line, it only takes the space that is required or necessary. Examples :- anchor tag, img tag, span tag, input tag etc
